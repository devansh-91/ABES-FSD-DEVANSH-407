Metro Do's

1. Stand on the left side of the escalator and let others pass from the right.

2. Offer your seat to elderly, differently-abled, or pregnant passengers.

3. Keep your belongings close and secure at all times.

4. Maintain silence and use earphones while listening to music.

5. Follow queue lines while entering or exiting the train.

Metro Don'ts

1. Don’t eat or drink inside metro trains.

2. Don’t block train doors or push while boarding.

3. Don’t play loud music or talk loudly on the phone.

4. Don’t litter inside trains or at stations.

5. Don’t lean on the train doors or sit on the floor.
